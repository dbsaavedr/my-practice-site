About Us

With over 30 years in the restaurant business, our family brings a deep passion for food and hospitality to Dutchman Grill & Oyster Bar. From Mexican kitchens to seafood favorites, our experience is rooted in bold flavor and tradition.

We’re a small, local family from Seabrook, Texas, proud to serve our community with fresh ingredients, honest cooking, and a laid-back coastal atmosphere.

From freshly shucked oysters to grilled specialties, everything we serve reflects the flavors and values we’ve built over generations—good food, done right.
